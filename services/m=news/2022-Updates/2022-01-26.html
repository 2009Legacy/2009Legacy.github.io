Fixing the bugs and training mages
  • Up to Legacy Update List
  • Refresh
  Summer
Website Developer
26-Janury-2022 00:08

A Back to back update to bring even more fixes to 2009scape. More focus on creating a structure within the development with some few changes.

Bot Fixes

  • Temporarily disabled green dragon bots pending economical changes.

Mage Training Arena

  • It is now possible to talk to the telekinetic guardian through the maze wall.
  • Asking for the price of an MTA reward no longer attempts to purchase said reward.
  • Graveyard bones now give the correct amount of bananas/peaches.
  • The number of telekinetic mazes solved now persist when exiting.

Some Bug Fixes

  • Re-import charm drop rates (some NPCs may have incorrectly had 100% charm drop rates).
  • Unicorn horns, snapegrass, limpwurt roots, and crushed nests from GWD minions now all be noted like wines of zamorak are.
  • Added server-wide announcements for godsword shards, d plate shards, steam battlestaff, and seercull.

The little things

  • Changed the Dragon claws drop rate to 1/256.
  • Changed the Dragon plate pieces drop rate to 1/1024.
  • Fixed abnormally frequent GS shard 3 drops from Balfrug Kreeath.
  • Fixed ruby bolt (e) sound effects.
