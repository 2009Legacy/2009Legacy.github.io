Wolf Whistle





Introduction


Introduction
There is a disturbance upstairs in an old summoner's house.

Pikkupstix needs help as an evil bunny captures his house!
Quest Information
Members only: What is membership?
Start point: Tavery's Summoning Shop
To Start: Speak to Pikkupstix
Quest Length: short
Minimum Requirements:
  • There are no requirements

To get started make your way to Taverly and speak with Pikkupstix in his house located next to the pet shop. When you get to him he mentions that there is a disturbance upstairs.

He warns you not to go upstairs but you ignore his warnings. You climb the ladder to find yourself staring at a giant bunny called a wolpertinger. As you notice the bunny you cower in fear and head back downstairs to figure out what you must do.

Pikkupstix tells you to go get two wolf bones to complete the pouch. So you make your way over to white wolf mountain to get the wolf bones. You find a body of his old friend and you search the body finding wolf bones. You bring the wolf bones back to Pikkupstix and he gives you the rest of the ingredients in order to make the spirit wolf. You use the key on the trap door and head down to this secret undergrown room. You head over to the obelisk and you make two spirit wolf pouches. You then use the pouch on the obelisk to bring up another menu to make scrolls. Right click the scroll and only turn one pouch into the scroll. Head back upstairs and speak with Pikkupstix. He then says you are ready so you make your way back up stairs.

Once you are upstairs, right click the pouch and press the summon option. You summon a spirit wolf and on the right of your screen will pop up a familiars menu which you can see your wolf. Click the special at the top left of the menu to use the howl ability on the wolpertinger. You'll notice a tornado attacking the wolpertinger making it dissapear.

Once you are back downstairs, Pikkupstix will congratulate you but tell you that you should renew your summoning points. So head back down stairs to the obelisk again. You then right click the obelisk and select to renew points.

After you renew your summoning point, you head back up stairs and speak with Pikkupstix one last time. He tells you that you now have access to the summoning skill and he gives you a load of gold charms. Congrats you have completed the quest!
