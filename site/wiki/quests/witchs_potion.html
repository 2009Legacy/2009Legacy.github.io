Witch's Potion





Introduction


Introduction
A magical Cook's Assistant

Help a witch make some soup but don't stir too fast.
Quest Information
Members only: What is membership?
Start point: Witch's house in Rimmington
To Start: Speak to the witch
Quest Length: short
Minimum Requirements:
  • There are no requirements

To get started you speak to the witch in her house in Rimmington. When you get to her ask about a quest you can do for her. She talks about needing ingredients for a potion.

Rats Tail

A Rats Tail can easily be obtained by killing a lv 1 rat which can be found all over Rimmington.

Onion

An onion can be found near Rimmington in a field near cabbages. Just head north of the witch's house towards the poh portal.

Burnt Meat





You can buy Raw beef from Wydin's Food Shop in Port Sarim. There is a ranged near by close to the fish shop where you can burn the meat. If you cook the meat, just use the cooked meat back on the ranged to burn it.

Eye of Newt

Head over the magic shop close by to buy an eye of newt from the shop.

Now head back to the witch back at her house and she will put the ingredients in her cauldron. You take a sip from the cauldron to get the dark magic rushing towards you. That's the end of the quest.
