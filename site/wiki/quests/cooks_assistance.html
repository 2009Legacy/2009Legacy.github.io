Cook's Assistant





Introduction


Introduction
A Cook in Distress

Help the Cook make a cake for the Duke.
Quest Information
Members only: What is membership?
Start point: Lumbridge Castle's Kitchen
To Start: Speak to Cook
Quest Length: short
Minimum Requirements:
  • There are no requirements

To get started make your way to the kitchen in Lumbridge castle's Kitchen.

He tells you that he needs to bake a cake for the Duke's birthday but is freaking out that he does not have any of the ingredients. You tell him that you'd be happy to help him gather all of the ingredients. He tells you that you will need to find an egg, bucket of milk, and a pot of flour.

You are able to find a bucket using the trap door in the kitchen and going downstairs towards the sink. The bucket will be located on the sink.Once you have a bucket head over to the cow pen over the bridge of the River Lum. Use the bucket on dairy cow and you will get milk.

Once you have the bucket of milk run across to the chicken coup and grab an egg you can find spawned on the ground. If one is not there right away it means someone else has probably picked it up. Just wait for one to spawn if it is not already there.

Head back over the bridge and head towards a wheat field. Head over into the windmill and grab the pot on the back table. Make your way up all the way to the top and use the wheat on the hopper. Once you have put it in the hopper pull the lever and head back downstairs. Once downstairs click on the mill to gather the flour.

Head back to the kitchen and speak with the cook. Once you have given him all the ingredients you have completed the quest.
