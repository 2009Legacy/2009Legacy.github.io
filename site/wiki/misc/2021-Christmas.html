Christmas 2021








Introduction

2009scape is celebrating their second Christmas event called 30 days of Christmas. Unlike the other events in 2009scape so far this event has nothing to do with any type of npc to turn an item in. Easter had the eggs in your basket while halloween had the candy. This is a bit different. You are able to do different activities to get christmas presents. These presents contain a variety of items in which have different chances of getting.
